Play Football

Prototype created at #datahackfest

  • See your local soccer fields. See who's playing and when!
  • Create a game by signing in with Facebook, the site will divide the teams for you
  • See which games have un-even teams and join their game
  • Works on any device! Use it on your iPhone, iPad or Android device
